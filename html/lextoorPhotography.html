Project Kickoff and Discovery

Client: VLC Media Player, the free and open-source, cross-platform multimedia player software used by millions worldwide.

Challenge: Reinvigorate a beloved brand for a global audience.

Objective: Design a website that delivers a consistent and contemporary brand experience, strengthens brand awareness, and solidifies relationships with users.

Role: UX/UI Design

Industry: Mircomobility

Duration: 4 Weeks

Key Objectives

Establish a Consistent Brand Identity

Deliver a Contemporary Brand Experience

User Research and Analysis

My journey began with deep user research. Understanding the needs, pain points, and aspirations of VLC's diverse user base was paramount. Through surveys, interviews, and usability testing, I unearthed valuable insights that formed the bedrock of the redesign.

User needs

Strengthening user engagement

Enhancing user experience

Elevating brand identity

Key Objective

Consistent Brand Identity

I've refreshed the design system while carefully preserving the original brand identity. I made subtle adjustments to the color palette, increasing its brightness to evoke a sense of contemporary freshness. For the typeface, I chose Avenri due to its neutral personality, which perfectly aligns with the brand's core values.

The Power of Symbols

By incorporating pictograms, I'm not just simplifying communication; but creating creating a more welcoming, inclusive, and ultimately, more successful digital space. They become the silent ambassadors of the brand, engaging users on a deeper level and forging a lasting connection.

So, I embrace the power of pictograms so everyone can understand, interact, and enjoy the best user experience possible.

Creating a positive emotional connection

I implemented a subtle and delightful movement to the website's UI, which helped to clarify interactions by reducing confusion and leading to a smoother, more intuitive experience. By breaking up static content, small animations helped me to add visual interest and prevent visual fatigue, keeping users engaged and focused.

The combination of clarity, engagement, and a touch of delight through micro-animations ultimately helped me create a website that feels more impactful and engaging.

From Wireframes to Reality:

The design process unfolded through iterative cycles of wireframing, prototyping, and user testing. Each iteration refined the user flow, optimized layouts, and ensured visual consistency with VLC's brand identity.

The final design strikes a balance between familiarity and innovation. Clean lines and a contemporary color palette uphold VLC's established brand identity, while user-centered features and intuitive interactions create a delightful user experience.

Experience Learned

This project was more than just a website redesign; it was a chance to shape the future of a brand. By putting user needs at the heart of the design process, I'm proud to have played a role in creating a website that empowers users, strengthens communities, and solidifies VLC's position as a global multimedia icon.
