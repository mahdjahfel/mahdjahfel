Project Kickoff and Discovery

Lextoor, an EV company focused on sustainable, affordable, and shared mobility, aims to build a strong digital presence reflecting its core values and mission. This involves crafting a user-friendly mobile app and website for a consistent, engaging experience on all devices.The digital platform will embody Lextoor's brand identity while ensuring a seamless user journey.

Role: UX/UI Designer

Industry: Mircomobility

Duration: 4 Weeks

Key Objectives

Establish a Consistent Brand Identity

Deliver a Multi-Device Experience

Create an Enjoyable User Experience

Embody Lextoor's Core Values

User Research and Analysis

I've applied the principles of Design Thinking in our work to deeply understand user needs, ideate solutions, prototype features, and continuously test and iterate, ensuring that our product aligns with user preferences and expectations.

Extensive research allowed me to empathize with users, identifying and solving multiple pain points they faced. This pivotal approach not only understood their needs but also crafted direct solutions, leading to a more satisfying and user-friendly product experience.

User needs

Comprehensive information about electric scooters

Easy comparison of different models and features

Create an Enjoyable User Experience

Key Objective

Consistent Brand Identity

The challenge — resided in crafting a design within the UX/UI realm that not only prioritized functionality but also encapsulated the brand's core values, personality, and mission, translating them effectively across varied experiential touchpoints.

The Solution — I tackled the challenge by using a structured design system, unifying the brand experience across platforms with a cohesive visual identity and interaction framework. This ensured consistency and scalability while seamlessly integrating the brand essence across interface interactions.

User Need

Info Accessibility Challenge

The challenge — provide detailed product info without overwhelming users while aligning with the desired brand personality for a unique market position.

The solution — involved using the grid system to present detailed product information efficiently. It optimized space, streamlined design, encouraged creativity, and supported iterative cycles for alignment with stakeholder objectives and user needs.

Key Objective

Multi-Device Experience

The challenge — was how to create a responsive and consistent design to ensure a multi-device experience that reflects the brand's personality, values, and mission.

The solution — To establish a cohesive and brand-aligned multi-device experience, I embraced the design system as a cornerstone of the project. This approach ensured consistency in the brand's visual identity across various devices and platforms. For responsiveness, the grid system proved its efficacy once again, enabling the creation of flexible and consistent designs that adapt seamlessly to different screen sizes.

User Need

Comparaison Tool

The Challenge —Our primary challenge was bridging the gap between user needs for seamless product comparison and maintaining a strong brand identity.

The Solution —Instead of reinventing the wheel, I opted for a highly effective design solution already adopted by industry leaders in similar situations. This not only saved me a considerable amount of time and effort but also provided a solid foundation. I then fine-tuned the design to reflect the brand identity through strategic elements like the primary color palette, signature typeface, and a robust grid system, ensuring coherence and visual impact.

Experience Learned

This project taught me the crucial balance between stakeholder goals and user needs. My multidisciplinary lens helped me tackle challenges from all angles, crafting solutions that met both, while AI tools like Bard & Chat GPT saved me time and boosted my workflow.
