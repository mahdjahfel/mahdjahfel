Project Kickoff and Discovery

The Goal: Creating an app that will serve as a secondary remote controller for the E-scooter. My job was to deliver an easy and enjoyable experience while achieving a consistent look and feel across different devices (phone and watch) that reflect the brand's identity and personality.

Role: UX/UI Designer / Motion Designer

Industry: Mircomobility

Duration: 4 Weeks

Key Objectives

Establish a Consistent Brand Identity

Deliver a Multi-Device Experience

Create an Enjoyable User Experience

Embody Lextoor's Core Values

The benefits of the grid system

The grid system was a fantastic tool that helped me bring order to chaos, boost readability, establish visual hierarchy, and ensure brand consistency. This resulted in a user experience that was not only meaningful but also aesthetically pleasing. It also offered me flexibility for creative expression while saving precious time and effort.

Micro-Interactions

After extensive testing for the most intuitive, seamless, and enjoyable experience, I selected test B. Test B restricts user movement to a single direction, eliminating other distractions and enabling users to interact with the product without conscious effort.

User Journey

Emphasizing both ease and enjoyment, I sculpted an account creation journey that seamlessly unfolds like a personalized story. Each stage invites users to discover new facets of our brand, culminating in a satisfying sense of belonging and anticipation for what's next.

The cerculare challenge

Bringing Lextoor Lite's minimal design to the active face posed unique challenges, particularly the constraint of the circular screen. To avoid dense, unreadable text columns, I employed a grid system, efficiently distributing secondary data around the circumference and the primary data at the center.

Mixing color, generous font size, and strategic spacing, I broke up information chunks, maximizing legibility and readability. This grid-based approach ensured optimal text proportions, contributing to a clean and effortlessly digestible user experience.

Rotary dial animation

Inspired by the mesmerizingly smooth animations of 60s phones, I crafted a rotary dial that delivers a delightful and intuitive user experience. Blending vibrant colors and captivating typography with the dial's nostalgic charm, I'm proud to have created more than just a functional, understandable, and usable product. This animation brings joy and excitement, pleasure and fun, and yes, beauty to people's lives.

Experience Learned

The project was truly fulfilling, allowing me to leverage my expertise as a UX/UI designer and motion designer. I applied these skills to create an enjoyable and user-friendly experience that seamlessly aligned with the stakeholders' objectives. Once again, I witnessed the power of wearing multiple hats and how it positively contributes to the overall success of a project.
